JSP/Servlet Session Mechanism

A few ways to maintain session between client/server is to use cookies as a webserver can assign unique session ID's to a user's cookie. Session mechanism for my website requires extensive setAttributes() and getAttributes() of the database using the POST method for my forms. To remove a user's session data, I used setMaxInactiveInterval(int interval) function which times out a session.
